Our Story

WHERE WE
CAME FROM

From a small group of students with a shared obsession to a nationally competing Baja SAE program. Here's how Sooner Offroad was built.

The Beginning

HOW IT ALL
STARTED

Sooner Offroad was founded by a group of University of Oklahoma engineering students who wanted to take their classroom skills into the real world — not in a lab, but on a race track.

The team started with limited resources and a lot of ambition. Early seasons were about learning the ropes of Baja SAE competition, understanding the ruleset, and building the team culture that would carry the program forward.

Over the years the team grew — more members, more experience, more capability. The shop got better tooling, the designs got more refined, and the results at competition reflected that growth.

Today Sooner Offroad is a full-featured engineering design team with dedicated subsystem leads, an established fabrication process, and a track record of finishing some of the most demanding endurance events in collegiate motorsport.
