The Crew

PEOPLE BEHIND
THE BUILD

Sooner Offroad runs on three Captains, six Leads, three Assistant Leads, and a growing roster of general members — all contributing hands-on to a vehicle that competes internationally. One Team, One Goal.

Leadership

THE CAPTAINS

Three equal co-captains lead the team — each with shared responsibility across all operations, and each owning a distinct domain that plays to their strengths. They collaborate on every major decision, from sponsorships to design to competition strategy.

WANT TO BUILD SOMETHING THAT RACES?

We're open to all OU students — throughout all engineering disciplines and beyond. No prior experience required. Just show up ready to work.
